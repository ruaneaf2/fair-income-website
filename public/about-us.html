The current levels of income support are inadequate

Our mission is to advocate for meaningful change in government policy regarding income support payments, aiming to increase the daily rate to from $54, to a minimum of $78.

We believe that every individual deserves to live with dignity and security, free from the constant worry of financial instability.

By amplifying the voices of those affected and engaging with policymakers, we seek to bring about systemic reform that addresses income inequality and breaks the cycle of entrenched poverty.

Join us in urging the government to increase income support payments. It's time to ensure that every individual and family across Australia can live in dignity and stability. Your voice is powerful—let's unite to create transformative change
